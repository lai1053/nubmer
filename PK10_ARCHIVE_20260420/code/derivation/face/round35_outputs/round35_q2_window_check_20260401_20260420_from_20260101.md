## Why
- The frozen round35 candidate layer itself uses `lookback_weeks = 26` for both the BS core and the OE enhancement.
- Restricting history to `2026-01-01` through `2026-04-19` leaves materially less than 26 complete historical weeks, so the retained components do not warm up.
- On top of that, the round35 daily gate still requires a trailing `40`-day window.

## Interpretation
- If you insist on initializing the current frozen window only from `2026-01-01`, then `2026-04-01` through `2026-04-20` are **not** window days.
- This does **not** mean the strategy judged those dates negatively under full-history deployment; it means the strategy cannot be validly initialized from such a short slice.
